Privacy Policy for meow.autos

Last updated: October 7, 2024

At meow.autos, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information gathered from users who visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit meow.autos, we may collect certain information, which includes:

  • Page URL: We track which specific pages are visited on our site.
  • Geolocation: If you grant permission, we collect your general geographic location (e.g., city or region).
  • Timestamp: The date and time when a page is visited.

2. How We Use the Data

The data we collect is used solely for analytical purposes. Specifically, we use this data to:

  • Identify which pages on our site are more popular.
  • Analyze patterns related to the time and location of visits to better understand traffic and improve user experience.

We do not use this data for any marketing or targeted advertising purposes.

3. No IP Tracking

We do not track or store your IP address. Any IP information collected by Google Analytics is anonymized, meaning we cannot personally identify you based on your IP address. The anonymization process ensures that your full IP address is never stored or logged by our systems.

4. No Marketing or Third-Party Sharing

The data we collect (page URL, timestamp, and geolocation) is not shared with third parties for marketing or advertising. The data is purely used for internal analysis to improve our website and user experience.

5. Use of Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors engage with meow.autos. Google Analytics provides anonymized data about website usage, such as page visits, geolocation, and time of visit.
